Raghunandan Tiwari Pushtakalaya and Club

It's for you.

Raghunandan Tiwari Pushtakalaya and Club was established in 1956 by Raghunandan Tiwari and Ramyatan Tiwari. Their vision was to make students of the area more educated and skilled.

Students from all backgrounds use this library and participate in club activities where they showcase their talents and skills.



President (Smt. Naina Shukla)

An entrepreneur and social worker dedicated to the progress of Raghunandan Tiwari Pushtakalaya and Club.

She continuously encourages students to read more books and participate in co-curricular activities.
