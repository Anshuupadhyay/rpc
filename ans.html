Raghunandan Tiwari Pushtakalaya and Club

It's for you.

Raghunandan Tiwari pushtakalaya and club was established by Raghunandan Tiwari and Ramyatan Tiwari in 1956.Their motto was to make the students of their area more educated and skilled.Students from all the background are making use of this library.They are participating in the events organised by club and are showing their hidden talents.




President(Smt. Naina Shukla)-

An entrepreneur and a social worker. She is busy in making Raghunandan Tiwari pustakalaya and Club progress every day. She always encourages students to read books as much they can and participate in co-curricular activities.
