Raghunandan Tiwari Pushtakalaya and Club

It's for you.

Raghunandan Tiwari Pushtakalaya and Club was established by Raghunandan Tiwari and Ramyatan Tiwari in 1956. Their motto was to make the students of their area more educated and skilled. Students from all backgrounds use this library and participate in the events organized by the club, showing their hidden talents.



President (Smt. Naina Shukla)

An entrepreneur and social worker. She works continuously to develop Raghunandan Tiwari Pushtakalaya and Club. She encourages students to read books and participate in co-curricular activities.
